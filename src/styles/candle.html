Description

Watch candles burn in this epicly crafted game.

Featuring 4 different candle styles ranging from Birthday Wishes to Elegant or Peace of Mind.

Each candle slowly burns a long descent until it reaches 0%...

THEN A NEW ONE SHOWS UP...

And you get to watch the candle all over again...

Do you have the patience?!

See how many candles you can watch (and how long you've been watching in one of the side-tabs)!

But be careful as shaking the device as well as blowing into the mic will cause it to be put out, requiring you to use one of your re-lights.
