Description

The app designed to help you write better essays.

This app is not intended to teach you what to write about in your essay, but rather go through the essential items in a well-written essay and pushes your essay's quality goes from good to excellent.

From Font to Transition phrases, and Point of View to Word Choice, The Essay Checklist has you covered when writing your next essay. All the items in the app are brief so you can get back to proofreading and amending your essay sooner. It's straight and to-the-point about what turns a regular essay into an awesome essay.

Even if you're someone who usually gets the best grades on your papers, this app is still worth checking out as you might learn a tip or two about compiling a nice piece of work.

Designed with the help of a high school English teacher, The Essay Checklist, is surely to be helpful in at least one of your essays and make you an overall better writer.
