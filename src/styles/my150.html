Description

Welcome to My150, the app where you can keep track of when you last contacted your closest 150 friends.

It's said that the human brain can only keep about 150 personal connections in its head before it starts to lose reliability.

This app helps you reach that goal and go beyond your 150 closest friends, family, and people you met in college.

Use it frequently, or infrequently.

My150 will show you when you last touched base.

Compatible with all devices and anything iOS 8+, you'll love how easy it is to keep in touch with your closest 150 contacts.
